\# PROJECT\_ROADMAP.md



Project Roadmap

Project: משתחררים (Mishtachrerim)



This document defines the development roadmap of the product.



The roadmap focuses on launching a \*\*working MVP quickly\*\* while building the foundation for future growth.



The roadmap is divided into phases.



---



\# 1. Development Philosophy



The goal is to launch a \*\*useful product quickly\*\*, not a perfect product.



Priorities:



1\. Decision Engine works

2\. SEO pages exist

3\. Quiz produces useful results

4\. Users can discover careers

5\. Course clicks and leads are possible



Everything else is secondary.



---

\# 7. SEO Roadmap



SEO growth will occur gradually.



---



\## Month 1–3



Publish foundational guides.



Example topics:

איך להשתמש בפיקדון  

מקצועות בלי תואר  



---



\## Month 3–6

\# 10. Product Risks



Key risks include:



Low quiz completion rate



Low trust in recommendations



Weak SEO performance



---

---



\# 11. Development Strategy
