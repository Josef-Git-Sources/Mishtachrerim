\# PROJECT\_MASTER\_DOC.md



Master Context Document  

Project: משתחררים (Mishtachrerim)



This document contains the core context of the project and must be read before any development work begins.



---



\# 1. Project Vision



"משתחררים" is a \*\*Decision Engine for Israeli discharged soldiers\*\*.



The product helps users understand \*\*what their next step after military service should be\*\*.



Possible outcomes include:



\- learning a profession

\- starting a career

\- choosing a training program

\- understanding first life steps after discharge



The product is NOT:



\- a job board

\- a government information portal

\- a blog



The product IS:



A \*\*guided decision tool\*\* that helps users move from uncertainty to a clear career direction.



---



\# 2. Core Product Concept



The experience should feel like:



\*\*A guided assistant for life after the army\*\*



Users are not browsing information.



They are \*\*guided through a decision flow\*\*.



---



\# 3. Core User Problem



Discharged soldiers often don't know:



\- what to do immediately after discharge

\- what rights they have

\- whether they should study or work

\- which profession fits them

\- how to use the army deposit



Existing information is fragmented and generic.



The product solves this by providing:



\- simple guidance

\- a structured quiz

\- career recommendations

\- training options



---



\# 4. Core Product Flow



The primary user flow:



Homepage / SEO  

→ Guide or Checklist  

→ Quiz  

→ Results (Top 3 Career Paths)  

→ Career Path Page  

→ Courses



Key principle:



\*\*Courses are shown only after career guidance.\*\*



---



\# 5. SEO Strategy



SEO is a \*\*primary acquisition channel\*\*.



The site will rank for queries such as:



\- what to do after the army

\- professions without a degree

\- how to use the army deposit

\- what to study after military service



SEO pages include:



\- Guides

\- Career Path pages

\- Editorial course pages

\- Course pages



These pages feed the product funnel.



---



\# 6. Career Path Layer



Career paths are the core layer between quiz and courses.



Career Path pages serve three roles:



1\. SEO landing pages  

2\. Trust pages explaining professions  

3\. Conversion bridges to courses



Initial career paths include:



\- QA Tester

\- Digital Marketing

\- Data Analyst

\- IT Support

\- Project Management

\- Graphic Design

\- Ecommerce

\- Network Technician

\- Full Stack Developer

\- UX/UI Designer



---



\# 7. Courses Layer



Courses are curated manually.



The platform is NOT an open marketplace.



Characteristics:



\- 10–30 courses

\- manually curated

\- editorial ratings

\- no user reviews

\- linked to career paths



Courses are shown primarily within career path pages.



A limited browse layer for courses also exists.



---



\# 8. Quiz System



The quiz helps match users with career paths.



Two quiz modes exist:



Quick Quiz  

5 questions



Deep Quiz  

10 questions



Each answer contributes scores to career paths.



The system returns the \*\*top 3 matching careers\*\*.



---



\# 9. Checklist Layer



Checklist provides practical post-discharge guidance.



Examples:



\- register with National Insurance

\- update health insurance

\- check discharge grant

\- use army deposit

\- preferred jobs



Checklist is lightweight and optionally saved for logged-in users.



---



\# 10. User Accounts



Registration is optional.



Users can view results without registering.



Registration enables:



\- saving results

\- saving courses

\- saving checklist progress



Authentication methods:



\- Google login

\- Email login



---

Affiliate commissions from course providers
