Intent Page Strategy

Project: משתחררים



Intent pages capture long-tail search queries.



These queries represent users with specific questions.



Examples:



איך להיות QA Tester

כמה מרוויח Data Analyst

איך להיכנס להייטק בלי תואר



Intent pages connect search demand to the decision engine.

PURPOSE OF INTENT PAGES



Capture long-tail SEO traffic.



Answer specific questions.
