This document defines the intent-driven SEO pages for the site.



Intent pages capture specific search queries where the user is looking for concrete answers.



These pages sit between guides and career pages in the SEO funnel.



SEO Funnel



Guide

→ Intent Page

→ Career Page

→ Course Page



Intent pages target long-tail search queries and high-intent questions.

INITIAL LAUNCH PLAN



The site should launch with approximately:



10 Guides

15 Intent Pages

10 Career Pages

10-20 Course Pages

3-5 Editorial Course Pages



This provides sufficient SEO surface area for search engines.



---



LONG TERM SEO EXPANSION



Target content scale:



30+ Guides

40+ Intent Pages

20+ Career Pages

50+ Course Pages

15+ Editorial Course Pages



This creates a strong organic traffic engine.



---



SEO PRINCIPLE



Intent pages capture search demand.



Career pages explain the profession.



Course pages convert users.



The quiz connects the entire system.
