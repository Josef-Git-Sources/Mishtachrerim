# SEO_STRATEGY.md

SEO Strategy
Project: משתחררים (Mishtachrerim)

This document defines the SEO strategy used to attract users to the Mishtachrerim platform.

The product relies heavily on organic search traffic.

SEO is the main acquisition channel for the platform.

The strategy focuses on capturing search intent related to:

Career exploration
Post-army planning
Professional training options

The goal is to guide users from search queries into the decision engine and ultimately toward career exploration and training programs.

---

# 1. SEO Philosophy

The SEO strategy follows three principles:

Capture intent
Build trust
Guide decisions

The platform should not behave like a generic blog.

Instead it should behave like:

A structured decision platform supported by SEO content.

Every SEO page must guide the user toward one of the following actions:

Take the quiz
Explore a career path
Explore courses

---

# 2. Core SEO Funnel

The primary SEO funnel is:

Search Query

↓

SEO Page

↓

Quiz or Career Page

↓

Courses

This funnel helps move users from:

Information
→ Direction
→ Action

---

# 3. Main SEO Page Types

The platform uses four primary SEO page types.

Guides
Intent Pages
Career Path Pages
Editorial Course Pages

These page types capture different types of search intent.

---

# 4. Guides

Route structure:

/guides/[slug]

Examples:

/guides/what-to-do-after-army
/guides/how-to-use-army-deposit
/guides/professions-without-degree

Purpose:

Guides capture broad informational queries.

Example queries:

"What to do after army"

"What jobs can I learn without a degree"

"What can I study with army deposit"

Guide pages should:

Explain the topic
Provide practical advice
Introduce possible career directions
Guide the user toward the quiz or career pages

---

# 5. Intent Pages

Route structure:

/career/[intent-slug]

Examples:

/career/how-to-become-qa
/career/qa-salary
/career/how-to-become-data-analyst

Purpose:

Intent pages target specific career-related searches.

Examples:

"How to become a QA tester"

"QA salary Israel"

"How to become data analyst"

These pages should:

Answer the search query directly
Explain the profession
Link to the relevant career page
Optionally link to the quiz

Typical flow:

Intent Page

↓

Career Page

↓

Courses

---

# 6. Career Path Pages

Route structure:

/career/[slug]

Examples:

/career/qa-tester
/career/data-analyst
/career/digital-marketing

Career pages are one of the most important SEO assets.

They serve several roles:

SEO landing pages
Trust-building pages
Decision pages
Course hubs

These pages should target queries such as:

"QA tester career"

"Digital marketing career"

"Data analyst job"

Each career page should include:

Career overview
Skills required
Salary information
Training options
Pros and cons
Recommended courses

---

# 7. Editorial Course Pages

Route structure:

/courses/editorial/[slug]

Examples:

/courses/editorial/best-qa-courses
/courses/editorial/top-digital-marketing-courses

Purpose:

Capture commercial-intent search queries.

Examples:

"Best QA course"

"Top digital marketing course"

"Best data analyst training"

These pages compare multiple courses and guide users toward training programs.

---

# 8. Course Pages

Route structure:

/courses/[slug]

Examples:

/courses/qa-bootcamp
/courses/data-analyst-online

Course pages are not primary SEO acquisition pages.

They mainly serve users who already know what they want.

These pages support conversion and referral traffic.

---

# 9. Checklist Page

Route:

/checklist

The checklist is a hybrid page.

It provides practical value and may capture SEO traffic for queries such as:

"What to do after army"

"Checklist after military service"

The checklist also acts as a product entry point.

---

# 10. Internal Linking Strategy

Internal linking is critical for SEO performance.

Important connections include:

Guides → Quiz
Guides → Career Pages

Intent Pages → Career Pages

Career Pages → Courses

Editorial Course Pages → Course Pages

Results Pages → Career Pages

Internal links should help users move naturally through the decision funnel.

---

# 11. Structured Data

Structured data should be added where relevant.

Possible schema types include:

Article
FAQPage
Course
BreadcrumbList

This helps search engines better understand the content.

---

# 12. Content Quality Principles

SEO content should be:

Clear
Helpful
Actionable
Structured

Avoid:

Thin content
Keyword stuffing
Generic articles

Each page should provide real value.

---

# 13. Keyword Targeting Strategy

Keyword targeting focuses on three main categories.

Informational queries

Examples:

what to do after army
jobs without degree
best professions after army

Career exploration queries

Examples:

qa tester career
data analyst career
digital marketing job

Commercial training queries

Examples:

best qa course
digital marketing course
data analyst training

Each category maps to a different page type.

---

# 14. SEO Performance Goals

The platform should aim to:

Capture long-tail career queries
Capture post-army planning queries
Capture course comparison queries

Over time the site should build topical authority around:

Career transitions
Professional training
Post-army career planning

---

# 15. Content Scaling Strategy

SEO growth should focus on scaling content in three layers.

Guide content expansion

More guides about post-army planning and career exploration.

Career intent expansion

More intent pages for each career path.

Course comparison expansion

More editorial pages comparing courses.

---

# 16. SEO and Product Integration

SEO should not exist separately from the product.

Every SEO page must guide users toward the product experience.

Possible calls to action include:

Take the quiz
Explore this career path
Compare training programs

The quiz acts as the central decision tool.

---

# 17. Long-Term SEO Strategy

As the site grows it should build authority around:

Career planning
Professional training
Skills development

Future SEO expansion may include:

More career paths
More training categories
More comparison pages

The goal is to become a trusted destination for career decisions after military service.
