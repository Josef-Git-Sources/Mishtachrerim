# SEO_STRATEGY.md

SEO Strategy
Project: משתחררים (Mishtachrerim)

This document defines the SEO strategy of the product.

Organic search is expected to be the **primary acquisition channel** for the platform.

The site is designed as an **SEO-first product combined with an interactive decision engine**.

SEO pages attract users from search engines and guide them into the product flow.

Typical flow:

Google Search
→ SEO Page
→ Quiz
→ Career Recommendation
→ Course Exploration
→ Conversion

---

# 1. SEO Strategy Overview

The SEO system is built around **four primary page types**:

1. Guides
2. Intent Pages
3. Career Path Pages
4. Editorial Course Pages

These pages form the core of the SEO content engine.

User flow from SEO:

Google Search
→ Guide Article OR Intent Page
→ Quiz CTA
→ Quiz Results
→ Career Page
→ Courses

---

# 2. Keyword Strategy

Keywords are grouped into clusters.

Each cluster maps to specific page types.

Main clusters:

After Army Planning
Career Discovery
No Degree Careers
Army Deposit
Course Comparison
Career Intent Queries

Each cluster corresponds to a specific type of page.

---

# 3. Keyword Cluster: After Army Planning

User intent:

Understanding what to do after military service.

Example keywords:

מה לעשות אחרי הצבא
מה ללמוד אחרי הצבא
עבודה אחרי צבא
כיוון קריירה אחרי צבא

Target pages:

Guides

Examples:

/guides/what-to-do-after-army
/guides/what-to-study-after-army

Goal:

Introduce the problem and guide users toward the quiz.

# 9. Core SEO Page Types

The website supports four primary SEO page types.

---

## Guides

Purpose:

Capture informational traffic.

Examples:

מה לעשות אחרי הצבא
מה ללמוד אחרי הצבא
מקצועות בלי תואר

CTA:

Take the quiz.

---

## Intent Pages

Purpose:

Answer specific career questions and guide users to the next step.

These pages target high-intent queries.

Examples:

איך להיות QA Tester
כמה מרוויח Data Analyst
איך להיכנס להייטק בלי תואר

Typical flow:

Intent Page
→ Career Page
→ Courses

Alternative flow:

Intent Page
→ Quiz

---

## Career Pages

Purpose:

Explain a specific career path.

Examples:

QA Tester
Data Analyst
Digital Marketing Specialist

CTA:

View courses and start training.

---

## Editorial Course Pages

Purpose:

Compare curated training programs.

Examples:

Best QA Courses
Best Data Analyst Courses

CTA:

Visit provider or request information.

---

# 10. Internal Linking Strategy

Every SEO page must link to:

Relevant guides
Relevant career paths
Relevant courses
Quiz entry point when appropriate

Typical linking flows:

Guide → Quiz → Career → Course

Intent → Career → Course

Guide → Career

---

# 11. Content Depth Requirements

SEO pages must provide real value.

Minimum content guidelines:

Guides

1500+ words

Intent pages

1000–1400 words

Career pages

1200+ words

Editorial course pages

1000+ words

Thin pages should be avoided.

---

# 12. Structured Data

Structured data should be implemented to improve search visibility.

Examples:

Article schema for guides

FAQ schema for intent pages

FAQ schema for career pages

Course schema for course pages

---

# 13. On-Page SEO Rules

Each SEO page must include:

SEO title
Meta description
Structured headings (H1 H2 H3)
Internal links
Clear introduction
Clear conclusion

Avoid:

Keyword stuffing
Thin content

---

# 14. Content Publishing Strategy

Initial launch should include:

10 guides
15 intent pages
10 career pages
10-20 course pages
3-5 editorial course pages

This provides enough content for search engines to index the site.

---

# 15. Traffic Funnel

SEO is the **top of the funnel**.

Flow:

SEO Page
→ Quiz
→ Career Page
→ Course Page
→ Conversion

The quiz is the bridge between content and monetization.

---

# 16. SEO Metrics

Key metrics to track:

Organic traffic
Keyword rankings
Page impressions
Quiz start rate from SEO pages
Career page views
Course clicks

---

# 17. Content Expansion Strategy

Over time the site should expand with:

More intent pages
More career pages
More course comparisons
More guides

All content must connect to the decision flow.

---

# 18. SEO Risks

Main risks:

High competition keywords.

Mitigation:

Focus on long-tail search queries.

Examples:

איך להיות QA בלי ניסיון
קורס QA עם פיקדון
איך להיכנס להייטק בלי תואר

---

# 19. Long-Term SEO Growth

Recommended long-term structure:

30+ guides
40+ intent pages
20+ career pages
50+ course pages
15+ editorial course pages

This creates a strong SEO footprint.

---

# 20. Core SEO Principle

SEO pages must not feel like generic content.

Every page must guide the user toward a **decision**.

The quiz and career pages are the central conversion bridges.
